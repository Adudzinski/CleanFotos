We operate the CleanPics mobile application ("the App"), which helps you find and delete similar or duplicate photos on your device. We are the data controller under GDPR (Regulation EU 2016/679).

2. The short version

CleanPics runs almost entirely on your device. Your photos are never uploaded to us or anyone else — all analysis happens locally on your phone. We have no servers, no accounts, and we never see your pictures. The only data that leaves your device is what Google's advertising and billing services need to show ads and process the optional purchase.

Deleting photos: when you choose to delete, the deletion is performed by your phone's operating system. Deleted photos typically move to your device's "Recently Deleted" / "Trash" album and can be restored there for about 30 days. CleanPics does not keep copies.

4. Ads (Google AdMob)

The free version of CleanPics shows ads through Google AdMob. To do this, Google may collect and process device and advertising identifiers and ad-interaction data, in accordance with Google's Privacy Policy. In the EU/EEA and UK, we show a consent prompt so you can choose between personalized and non-personalized ads. You can remove ads entirely with the one-time CleanPics Pro purchase.

5. Purchases & notifications

Pro purchase is handled by Google Play Billing. We never see or store your payment-card details. Notifications (the optional monthly cleanup reminder) are scheduled locally on your device — no push servers and no data are involved. You can turn them off in Settings or in your device's notification settings.
