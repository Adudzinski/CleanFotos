## ASO tips for the Play Console

- Put the strongest keywords in the **title** and **first 2 lines** of the full
  description (Google weights those most).
- Don't keyword-stuff — the paragraphs above read naturally while still
  covering the terms. Keep it that way to avoid policy issues.
- Update the description when you add features; fresh listings rank better.
- Ask happy users to rate (now built into the app — Settings → Rate CleanPics,
  plus the in-app prompt after a cleanup). Ratings strongly affect ranking.
- Localize the short + full description into the app's 6 languages for more
  organic reach (I can produce these on request).
